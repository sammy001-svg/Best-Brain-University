Transfer to BBIU

At Best Brains International University, we welcome students from all academic backgrounds. If you are looking to finish your degree in a multicultural, innovative environment, BBIU is the perfect place for you. We recognize the hard work you've already put in and strive to make the transfer process as smooth as possible.

Our dedicated transfer advisors will work with you one-on-one to evaluate your credits and ensure you stay on track for graduation.

1. Initial Inquiry

Reach out to our admissions team to discuss your current academic standing and goals.

2. Transcript Review

Submit your official transcripts from your current/previous institution for credit evaluation.

3. Admission Decision

Once evaluated, you'll receive an admission offer and a breakdown of transferred credits.

Eligibility & Requirements

Minimum GPA

Transfer applicants should typically have a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or higher from their previous institution.

Credit Policy

Generally, we accept credits for courses passed with a 'C' grade or better from accredited institutions.

Good Standing

Applicants must be in good academic and disciplinary standing at their last attended university.

Evaluation Time

Transcript evaluations usually take 7-10 business days after all documents are received.

Personalized Support for Every Transfer

Our Transfer Admissions team is here to help you navigate your unique journey. We provide personalized credit maps and financial aid counseling tailored to transfer students.
