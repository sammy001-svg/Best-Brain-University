About Our University

A Few Words About the University

Best Brains International University (BBIU) is a private institution of higher learning located in Liberia, West Africa. Established in 2018 and opened in 2021, BBIU is accredited by the National Commission on Higher Education of Liberia to grant a bachelor's degree. The university offers undergraduate degree programs in Education, Business Administration, Liberal Arts, and Information Technology. BBIU is also involved in various community outreach programs and character-building initiatives to foster a multicultural environment and promote global citizenship.
