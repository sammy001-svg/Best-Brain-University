About Our University

Welcome to Best Brains International University

Best Brains International University (BBIU) is a private institution of higher learning located in Liberia, West Africa. Established in 2018 and opened in 2021, BBIU is accredited by the National Commission on Higher Education of Liberia to grant a bachelor's degree.

The university offers undergraduate degree programs in Education, Business Administration, Liberal Arts, and Information Technology. BBIU is also involved in various community outreach programs and character-building initiatives to foster a multicultural environment and promote global citizenship.

Our Vision

Best Brains International University seeks to be a premier multicultural, educational, and technical training institution, preparing young people to develop the entrepreneur spirit and attitude needed to contribute meaningfully to the nation building process of Liberia and the world at large.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear and compelling: BBIU exists to provide quality tertiary education that will build the mental capacity and moral character of our students in order to equip them for quality and relevant profession so as to enhance their meaningful participation in the nation building process of the society.
