Admission Requirements

We select students based on their academic potential, personal character, and readiness to thrive in our rigorous academic environment.

Academic Criteria

  • High School Diploma or equivalent.
  • Minimum aggregate score in WAEC/WASSCE (WASSCE Preferred).
  • Proficiency in English (Both written and oral).
  • Satisfactory results in BBIU Entrance Examination.

Required Documents

  • Official high school transcripts (original copies).
  • Valid national ID or Passport.
  • Two letters of recommendation from former teachers.
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs.

Personal Statement

Prospects are required to submit a 500-word essay detailing:

  • Why you chose Best Brain International University.
  • Your career aspirations and academic goals.
  • How you plan to contribute to our campus community.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I expect an admission decision?
Generally, admission decisions are sent out via email 2-3 weeks after your interview process is complete and all supporting documents have been verified.
Is the BBIU entrance exam mandatory for all applicants?
Yes, all prospective undergraduate students must sit for our entrance examination to assess their quantitative and verbal reasoning skills.
Can I apply for multiple programs at once?
You can indicate up to two choices of programs in your application. Your primary choice will be considered first based on your qualifications.
What is the application fee?
The current undergraduate application fee is $30 USD (or equivalent in Liberian Dollars), which is non-refundable.
