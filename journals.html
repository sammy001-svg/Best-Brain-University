Submissions

Call for Papers & Guidelines

BBIU Journals invites researchers, academics, and professionals to submit original research articles, review papers, and case studies for our upcoming issues. We maintain a rigorous double-blind peer-review process to ensure the highest academic standards.

  • Manuscripts must be formatted according to APA 7th Edition guidelines.
  • Submissions should not exceed 8,000 words (excluding references).
  • Include an abstract of 150-250 words and 4-6 keywords.
  • All figures and tables must be embedded within the text.
