Administrative Pillars

University Directorates

The various directorates at BBIU work collaboratively to support the academic mission, manage university operations, and provide essential services to the campus community.

Directorate of Academic Affairs

Responsible for curriculum development, academic policies, quality assurance, and coordinating the activities of all academic departments and faculties.

Directorate of Student Affairs

Dedicated to enhancing the student experience through counseling, career services, extracurricular activities, and maintaining student welfare.

Directorate of Administration & Finance

Oversees human resources, financial planning, budgeting, procurement, and the general administrative operations of the university.

Directorate of International Programs

Facilitates global partnerships, manages international student admissions, and coordinates study abroad programs and international research collaborations.

Best Brain International University
