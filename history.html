The Early Years (2018 - 2021)

Best Brains International University firmly believe that learning is continuous and thus, we subscribe to the concept of collaborative and lifelong learning. Each applicant is reviewed carefully based on application requirements as defined and stipulated by the university.

In choosing prospective students, BBIU does not discriminate on the basis of race, sex, religion, national origin or personal disabilities.

Accreditation and Growth

A major milestone was achieved when BBIU received official accreditation by the National Commission on Higher Education of Liberia to grant bachelor's degrees. This validation cemented BBIU's status as a recognized institution of quality tertiary education.

Today, the university rapidly expanded its offerings to include comprehensive undergraduate degree programs in Education, Business Administration, Liberal Arts, and Information Technology.
